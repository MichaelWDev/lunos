Mission

ENCRYPTION

Although not implement yet, Advanced Encryption Standard (AES) 256 will be used to encrypt your data.

The United States government uses AES 256 for security. So we thought: If governments can use it, why can't we?

&

PRIVACY

Most applications store your information and sell it. We are never going to do that.

The only things we save in a database are encrypted message history and bug reports.
